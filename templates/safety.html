Safety Guidelines

Important: These guidelines exist to keep our community safe and supportive. All users must follow these rules to maintain their account access.

Personal Information Protection

What Information to Keep Private

To protect yourself online, never share the following information publicly on SynapseHub:

  • Your full real name (first name only is fine)
  • Home address, school address, or specific location details
  • Phone numbers or personal contact information
  • Social media accounts or external platform usernames
  • Financial information, including family income details
  • Photos that show identifying information (school uniforms, house numbers, etc.)

Safe Sharing Practices

You can safely share:

  • Your first name or a preferred nickname
  • General location (city or region, not specific addresses)
  • Your interests and entrepreneurial goals
  • Skills and experiences relevant to business
  • Your age (required for platform access)

Communication Guidelines

Appropriate Interactions

All communication on SynapseHub should be:

  • Respectful: Treat others with kindness and professionalism
  • Business-focused: Keep conversations related to entrepreneurship, learning, and collaboration
  • Constructive: Provide helpful feedback and support to fellow entrepreneurs
  • Age-appropriate: Remember that all users are between 13-18 years old

Prohibited Communication

The following behaviors are strictly forbidden:

  • Bullying, harassment, or intimidation of any kind
  • Sharing or requesting personal contact information
  • Romantic or sexual content or conversations
  • Attempting to meet other users in person
  • Using inappropriate language or offensive content
  • Spam, promotional content, or unrelated business pitches

Meeting and Collaboration Safety

Virtual Collaboration Only

SynapseHub is designed for online collaboration only. We strongly recommend:

  • Keeping all project work and communication on the platform
  • Using video calls only through approved platforms (with parental permission)
  • Never arranging to meet other users in person
  • Including parents/guardians in any external communication decisions
Never Meet Users in Person: Online friendships should stay online for your safety. If someone suggests meeting in person, report this to our safety team immediately.

Working with Mentors

All mentors are verified adults, but safety remains important:

  • Keep all mentor conversations focused on business and learning
  • Report any inappropriate behavior immediately
  • Feel free to involve parents/guardians in mentor relationships
  • Remember that mentorship is about guidance, not personal friendship

Intellectual Property and Ideas

Protecting Your Ideas

While sharing ideas is encouraged, protect yourself by:

  • Only sharing concepts you're comfortable making public
  • Keeping detailed proprietary information private
  • Understanding that public posts can be seen by all users
  • Documenting your ideas with dates and descriptions

Respecting Others' Ideas

When you see others' ideas:

  • Respect their intellectual property rights
  • Don't copy or steal concepts shared by others
  • Provide credit when building on someone else's idea
  • Ask permission before using someone's specific concept

Reporting and Support

When to Report

Report any situation that makes you uncomfortable, including:

  • Someone asking for personal information
  • Inappropriate messages or behavior
  • Bullying or harassment
  • Attempts to communicate outside the platform
  • Suspicious or concerning behavior of any kind

How to Report

You can report issues in several ways:

  • Use the Report Button: Available on all posts, profiles, and messages
  • Email Safety Team: safety@synapsehub.com for immediate attention
  • Contact Support: support@synapsehub.com for general concerns
  • Talk to Parents: Always feel comfortable involving parents/guardians
Your Report Matters: All reports are taken seriously and reviewed within 24 hours. Reporting helps keep our entire community safe.

Parental Involvement

Encouraging Parent Participation

We encourage parents and guardians to:

  • Review these safety guidelines with their children
  • Monitor their child's activity on the platform
  • Ask questions about projects and collaborations
  • Contact us with any concerns or questions
  • Celebrate their child's entrepreneurial journey

Parent Resources

Parents can contact us at parents@synapsehub.com for:

  • Questions about platform safety measures
  • Guidance on supporting their young entrepreneur
  • Reporting concerns about their child's interactions
  • Understanding the educational value of our programs

Platform Security

Account Security

Keep your account secure by:

  • Using a strong, unique password
  • Not sharing your login credentials with anyone
  • Logging out when using shared computers
  • Reporting any suspicious account activity immediately

Our Security Measures

SynapseHub protects users through:

  • Content moderation and automated safety filters
  • Regular review of all reported content
  • Verification processes for mentors and adult users
  • Secure data encryption and privacy protection
  • 24/7 monitoring for inappropriate content

Consequences for Violations

Enforcement Actions

Violating these guidelines may result in:

  • Warning: For minor first-time violations
  • Temporary Suspension: For repeated or moderate violations
  • Permanent Ban: For serious violations or repeated offenses
  • Legal Action: For illegal activities or serious safety violations
Remember: These guidelines exist to protect everyone in our community. When in doubt, choose the safer option and reach out for guidance.
