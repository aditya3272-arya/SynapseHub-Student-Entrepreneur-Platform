How Can We Help You?

Quick Start: New to SynapseHub? Check out our getting started guide below to make the most of your entrepreneurial journey.

Getting Started

Creating Your Account

Q: How do I sign up for SynapseHub?

A: Click the "Get Started" button on our homepage, fill in your details (username, email, password, and age), and you're ready to go! Make sure you're between 13-18 years old to join our community.

Setting Up Your Profile

Q: What should I include in my profile?

A: Add your interests, skills, and entrepreneurial goals. This helps other students find you for potential collaborations. Don't forget to upload a profile picture!

Your First Idea Post

Q: How do I share my startup idea?

A: Go to the "Ideas" section, click "Share New Idea," and describe your concept. Include what problem you're solving, your target audience, and what kind of teammates you need.

Platform Features

Daily Quiz System

Q: How do daily quizzes work?

A: Each day, you'll receive a new entrepreneurship question covering topics like business strategy, marketing, finance, and leadership. Answer correctly to earn points and track your learning progress.

Q: What happens if I miss a day?

A: No worries! You can catch up on missed quizzes for up to 7 days. Your streak will reset, but you won't lose any learning opportunities.

Team Formation

Q: How do I find potential co-founders?

A: Browse other students' profiles in the "Find Teammates" section. You can filter by skills, interests, and availability. Send collaboration requests to students whose skills complement yours.

Q: What makes a good team request?

A: Be specific about your idea, explain what role you need filled, and mention why you think they'd be a great fit. Personal, thoughtful messages get better responses than generic ones.

Mentorship Program

Q: How do I connect with a mentor?

A: Once you've been active for 2 weeks and completed at least 10 daily quizzes, you can apply for mentorship. Browse available mentors and send a request explaining your goals and challenges.

Idea Showcase

Q: Will my ideas be stolen?

A: We take intellectual property seriously. All users agree to respect others' ideas, and you retain full ownership of your concepts. Share only what you're comfortable making public.

Account & Technical Issues

Password & Login

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and we'll send you a reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within 5 minutes.

Profile Settings

Q: How do I change my age on my profile?

A: Contact our support team at support@synapsehub.com with proof of your correct age. We'll update it for you to maintain platform integrity.

Notifications

Q: How do I control what notifications I receive?

A: Go to Settings > Notifications to customize your email and in-app notification preferences. You can choose to receive updates about team requests, mentor messages, and daily quizzes.

Community Guidelines

Appropriate Behavior

Q: What kind of behavior is expected on SynapseHub?

A: We maintain a supportive, respectful environment. Be kind, constructive in feedback, and professional in all interactions. Bullying, harassment, or inappropriate content will result in account suspension.

Reporting Issues

Q: How do I report inappropriate behavior or content?

A: Use the "Report" button on any post or profile, or email us directly at safety@synapsehub.com. We review all reports within 24 hours and take appropriate action.

Success Tips

Pro Tips for Success:
  • Complete your daily quizzes consistently to build entrepreneurial knowledge
  • Engage with others' ideas by providing thoughtful feedback
  • Be open about your skill gaps - it helps you find the right teammates
  • Ask mentors specific questions rather than seeking general advice
  • Participate in community discussions to build your network

Still Need Help?
