Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 05, 2025

Last Updated: September 05, 2025

Important: This policy explains how SynapseHub collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using our platform, you agree to these practices.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account and use SynapseHub, you provide us with:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password, and age
  • Profile Information: Bio, interests, skills, entrepreneurial goals, and profile photo (optional)
  • Content: Ideas you share, quiz responses, comments, and messages
  • Communication: Messages to other users, mentor interactions, and support requests

Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our platform, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Performance Data: Quiz scores, learning progress, engagement metrics
  • Cookies: Small files that help us remember your preferences and login status

Information We Don't Collect

We never collect:

  • Social Security Numbers or government identification
  • Financial information or payment details (our platform is free)
  • Location data beyond general geographic region
  • Information about your family or household
  • Biometric data or health information

How We Use Your Information

Platform Operation

We use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Provide personalized learning experiences
  • Connect you with potential teammates and mentors
  • Track your educational progress and achievements
  • Improve our platform features and user experience

Communication

We may use your email address to send:

  • Account verification and password reset emails
  • Notifications about team requests and mentor messages
  • Platform updates and new feature announcements
  • Safety alerts and important policy changes
  • Educational content and entrepreneurship tips (opt-in only)

Safety and Security

We use information to:

  • Verify user ages and maintain a safe environment for minors
  • Monitor for inappropriate content and behavior
  • Investigate reported safety violations
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal requirements and law enforcement requests

Information Sharing

What We Share Within the Platform

Other SynapseHub users can see:

  • Your username and profile information you choose to make public
  • Ideas and content you post publicly
  • Comments and feedback you provide on others' ideas
  • General learning achievements and badges (not specific scores)

What We Don't Share

We never share with other users:

  • Your email address or contact information
  • Private messages or communications
  • Specific quiz scores or detailed learning analytics
  • Account security information

Third-Party Sharing

We may share limited information with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that help us operate the platform (email delivery, hosting)
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect user safety
  • Business Transfers: If SynapseHub is acquired (users would be notified)
  • Parental Requests: Parents/guardians may request information about their minor child's account
We Never Sell Your Data: SynapseHub will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

Data Security

How We Protect Your Information

We implement multiple security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Access Controls: Only authorized employees can access user data
  • Regular Audits: Security practices are reviewed and updated regularly
  • Secure Hosting: Data is stored on secure, industry-standard servers
  • Monitoring: We continuously monitor for unauthorized access attempts

Your Role in Security

Help keep your account secure by:

  • Using a strong, unique password
  • Not sharing your login credentials
  • Logging out on shared devices
  • Reporting suspicious activity immediately

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Update: Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your content and data in a portable format
  • Restrict: Limit how we process certain types of your data

Parental Rights

Parents and guardians of users under 18 can:

  • Review their child's personal information
  • Request updates or corrections to their child's data
  • Delete their child's account
  • Restrict certain data collection or sharing
  • Receive copies of this privacy policy and any updates

Data Retention

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain different types of data for varying periods:

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account or 2 years of inactivity
  • Learning Progress: 5 years to support long-term educational tracking
  • Public Content: Until you delete it or your account is deleted
  • Safety Reports: 7 years to maintain community safety records
  • Technical Logs: 90 days for security and performance monitoring

Deletion Process

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal information are immediately hidden
  • Data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for research and improvement
  • Legal compliance may require retaining certain records longer

International Users

Data Transfers

SynapseHub operates globally, which means:

  • Your data may be transferred to and stored in the United States
  • We comply with international data protection laws
  • Appropriate safeguards are in place for cross-border transfers
  • Users in the EU have additional rights under GDPR

Cookies and Tracking

Types of Cookies We Use

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Required for platform functionality and security
  • Preferences: Remember your settings and customizations
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with our platform
  • Performance: Monitor and improve platform speed and reliability

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Your browser settings (though this may affect platform functionality)
  • Our cookie preference center (available in account settings)
  • Opting out of non-essential cookies during registration

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Special Protections for Minors

Because all our users are under 18, we provide enhanced privacy protections:

  • Minimal data collection focused only on educational purposes
  • No behavioral advertising or marketing to minors
  • Parental rights to review and delete their child's information
  • Additional safety measures and content moderation
  • Regular privacy training for all staff handling user data

Changes to This Policy

How We Handle Updates

When we update this privacy policy:

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top will be changed
  • Users will be notified via email and platform notification
  • Major changes will include a 30-day notice period
  • Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of changes
  • Previous versions will be archived and available upon request

Contact Information

Privacy Questions and Requests

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@synapsehub.com
  • Response Time: Within 30 days of receipt
  • Parental Inquiries: parents@synapsehub.com
  • Data Requests: Include your username and specific request details
Questions? We're committed to transparency about our privacy practices. Don't hesitate to reach out if you need clarification about how we handle your information.
